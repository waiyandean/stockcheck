How to submit a product count
Follow these steps each time you do a daily product count. The whole process takes about 2 minutes.
Step by step
1
Open Stock Check
Open the Stock Check page on your phone or device. You will see the start screen with a logo and the heading Start a session.
2
Fill in your session details
Enter today's date, select your site from the dropdown, and type your name in the Staff name field.

All three fields must be filled in before you can continue.
3
Tap "Products only"
For a daily product count, tap the Products only button — Finished goods count — daily.

Only tap Full count when doing the weekly ingredient count as well.
The start screen — fill in all three fields, then tap Products only
4
Count your products and enter the numbers
You will see a list of all finished products. For each one, enter:

 • Cases — the number of full sealed cases
 • Packets / pouches — any open or loose units

If there are none for a product, you can leave that field blank.
The product list
Tap a field to enter a number
5
Tap "Submit stock check"
Once all products are filled in, scroll to the bottom and tap Submit stock check.

The form will save and sync to Google Sheets automatically. You will be taken back to the start screen when it is done.
Scroll to the bottom and tap Submit stock check
Common mistake to avoid
⚠ Do not enter counts into the Setup page
The Stock Setup page is for managers to configure product names and settings — it is not where you enter your daily counts. If you see fields labelled Product name, Packet label, or Minimum stock, you are in the wrong place.

Go back to Stock Check and start from Step 1.
If you see Edit and ✕ buttons, you are in Stock Setup — go back
✓ You are in the right place if you see…
A list of your products with Cases and Packets number inputs, and a black Submit stock check button at the bottom of the page.
